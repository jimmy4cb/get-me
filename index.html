About us

Lakeside Adventure Community is a people-powered eco-adventure and wellness movement based in Kisumu, Kenya. We bring together hikers, cyclists, campers, and nature lovers for meaningful outdoor experiences that support local families and protect natural habitats.

  • Guided hikes, cycling tours, and camping trips
  • Community-based eco-tourism with conservation benefits
  • Local culture, wildlife, and scenic lakeside adventures
  • Safe, welcoming experiences for first-time explorers

What we offer
